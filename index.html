Steven Boada is a sixth year physics doctoral student at Texas A&M University with specific research in astrophysics working under the direction of Dr. Casey Papovich. His research activities include galaxy clusters in large survey data, IFU observations of rich clusters, and galaxies at z = 2. A complete list of his publications can be found on the SAO/NASA Astrophysics Data System. He hopes to graduate Spring, 2016.

In addition to on going research, he is an active developer on astLib, a suit of astronomy related python modules, posts code he finds useful to GitHub, and rides his bike around the town.
